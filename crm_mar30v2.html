Connect Your Google Calendar

To display your calendar here, you need to get your personal Google Calendar embed link. It takes about 2 minutes and only needs to be done once.

How to get your embed link:
  1. Open Google Calendar in your browser
  2. Click the ⚙️ gear icon → Settings
  3. Click your calendar name on the left sidebar
  4. Scroll down to "Integrate calendar"
  5. Copy the "Public URL to this calendar" or the Embed code URL
  6. Paste it below and click Connect
